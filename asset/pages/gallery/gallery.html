ABOUT ASHES

It all dates back to 2006 when Evan, Rafsan & Bijoy, three friends discussed about how deep they can go with their passion which is music and how they can make it to the top. Together, these three passionate men created Ashes. Since then, it's been an unforgettable journey for these men doing what they love. Soon Towhid and Tanim joined in on the act. But it was only 3 years later, during 2009 that Ashes begun to work seriously on their own compositions. Their 1st mixed album was released on 2010, 5th November. Due to study issues, Tawhid & Tanim had to leave the band. In October 2011, Adnan joined the band as keyboardist & Amir Khan as manager. Later on, Turjo joined as the bassist to complete the lineup!
